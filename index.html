About Me



Hi, I'm Sven Luttik, a passionate and results-driven UX Designer dedicated to crafting seamless, user-centered experiences. With a deep understanding of design principles and a strong focus on usability, I strive to transform complex challenges into intuitive and engaging solutions. My approach to design is rooted in empathy—understanding the needs, behaviors, and pain points of users to create products that are not only visually appealing but also functional and accessible. I combine research, design thinking, and iterative testing to ensure every decision I make enhances the user journey.



Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with diverse teams and clients, gaining expertise in everything from wireframing and prototyping to user research and interaction design. Whether it’s improving an existing product or designing a new one from the ground up, I aim to deliver solutions that are both impactful and meaningful. When I’m not designing, I’m always exploring the latest trends in the UX/UI world, constantly learning and evolving to stay at the forefront of the field. Let’s create something amazing together!

TCG Nexus

TCG Nexus is the ultimate app for Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) collectors and enthusiasts. With TCG Nexus, you can easily manage, track, and assess the value of your Pokémon card collection.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting out, the app allows you to discover the current market value of each card, organize your collection, and stay up-to-date on trends in the Pokémon TCG community. Unlock the full potential of your collection with TCG Nexus – your one-stop hub for all things Pokémon cards!
